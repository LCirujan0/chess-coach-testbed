Where KnightPath is headed

Roadmap

KnightPath is built around three loops: a daily loop that keeps you training, a review loop that turns every game you play into lessons, and a mastery loop that makes sure what you learn actually sticks. Here is where each loop stands, and what is coming next.

Updated 10 June 2026
Just shipped
  • A real welcome: tell us your Chess.com username and your goal, and the coach analyses your last 20 games while you wait, then shows you three things Chess.com won’t
  • Your progress follows you to any device, and the whole app aims at your goal, not a generic 1500
  • Game-by-game performance: see the level you actually played at in each game, and what the trend means
  • A coach that remembers you, debriefs your sessions, and walks you through each game’s key moments
  • New here? Every trainer now explains itself the first time you open it
Loop 1 The daily loop, a session a day, built from your own games Live
Open the app, and a session is waiting: your real mistakes as puzzles, the patterns due for review, and the areas where you leak the most points. Finish it, keep your streak.
  • ✓
    A daily session assembled from your recent mistakes, with a goal you choose (casual, regular, serious) and a streak with freezes, life happens, your streak survives it.
  • ✓
    Spaced review: puzzles you missed come back quickly, ones you have mastered return just before you would forget them.
  • ✓
    A coach beside every board that guides you with questions, pitched to your rating, and never hands you the answer.
  • ✓
    When you finish, the coach debriefs your session: what you were sharp on, what slipped, and what tomorrow should look like.New
  • ✓
    Your streak, puzzles and progress sync across all your devices.New
Loop 2 The review loop, every game you play becomes a lesson Live
Sync after you play and KnightPath re-analyses every move. The moments that decided the game become things you can understand, then drill.
  • ✓
    Game Review replays your game and jumps you straight to the key moments, the coach explains each one in plain language.New
  • ✓
    Every mistake is tagged with its tactical theme (fork, pin, back rank…), with a one-tap "drill this theme" to practise it on the spot.
  • ✓
    Insights shows where you actually lose rating: by game phase, by move number, and your rating trajectory over time.
Loop 3 The mastery loop, skills that build on each other Live
Beyond fixing mistakes: a set of trainers that build lasting chess skill, with visible proof you are improving.
  • ✓
    Tactics drills by theme and difficulty, topped up from a 10,000-puzzle library matched to your rating.New
  • ✓
    Endgames: play 20 essential positions out against the computer, and learn to judge at a glance whether a position is winning, drawn or lost.
  • ✓
    An openings trainer with move-by-move explanations of why, on a spaced-repetition schedule, plus a daily board-vision warm-up.
  • ✓
    Mastery you can see: milestones when you conquer a pattern, and week-by-week progress charts for every training type.New
